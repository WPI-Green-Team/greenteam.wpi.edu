WPI Green Team

Meetings during A-term will be at 5PM in SL 105 on Mondays

The WPI Green Team is a student run organization dedicated to making WPI a more sustainable place and educating the WPI community about sustainability. The Green Team has several major annual events, including: The Waste Audit, where we sort through campus waste to analyze WPI’s waste and recycling practices; The Electric Vehicle Show, where we bring efficient vehicles to campus and sponsor test drives; The E-Waste Drive, where we help community members safely dispose of large electronic waste; and Recyclemania, where we take part in a national competition dedicated to raising recycling rates and awareness on campus. The Green Team also oversees the Gompei’s Gears Bike Share, which promotes bicycling as a healthy and sustainable commuting and recreation option. The Green Team holds many minor events throughout the year, generally dedicated to raising sustainability awareness among students.

Our Goals

  • Promote a sustainable environment at WPI and in the Worcester community.
  • Work with students and administration on topics of sustainability and environmental protection
  • Engage with the WPI community to educate about sustainable actions
  • Encourage students to take an active role in sustainability at WPI
  • Maintain Gompei’s Gears bikeshare
