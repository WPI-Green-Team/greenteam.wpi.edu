Waste Audit 2016 Results

Gordon Library

Gordon Library had a true recycling rate of 12% and a hauler's weight recycling rate of 20%. The total potential recycling rate is 22%.

The trash consisted of 54.6% plain trash, 25.1% food waste, 8% plastic, 6.7% liquid, 3.8% paper, 1.1% interesting items, and 0.6% cardboard.

The recycling bins consisted of 26% plastic, 26% paper, 21.7% plain trash, 17.3% liquid, 5.7% cardboard, 2.2% food waste, and 1.1% interesting items.

Campus Center

The Campus Center had a true recycling rate of 12% and a hauler's weight recycling rate of 16%. The total potential recycling rate is 22%.

The trash consisted of 61.3% plain trash, 23.1% food waste, 8.5% plastic, 3.9% liquid, 1.3% paper, 1.3% cardboard, and 0.6% interesting items.

The recycilng bins consisted of 39.8% cardboard, 27.9% plastic, 16.2% plain trash, 9.7% liquid, 6.3% paper, 0% food waste, and 0% interesting items.

Daniels Hall

Daniels Hall had a true recycling rate of 20% and a hauler's weight recycling rate of 34%. The total potential recycling rate is 33%.

The trash consisted of 61.8% plain trash, 8.4% food waste, 7.8% liquid, 7.3% cardboard, 5.8% interesting items, 4.6% plastic, and 4.3% paper.

The recycling bins consisted of 29.3% plastic, 24.3% plain trash, 17.4% paper, 11.8% cardboard, 10.3% food waste, 4.4% interesting items, and 2.5% liquid.
